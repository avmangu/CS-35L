gitk-screenshot.png Subwindow Role Description

TOP:
The window on the top left depicts a simple tree of all the commits made. On the
immediate right is a list of all the commits made over the past several
years. Each merge date is accompanied by a name and email address on the
individual who made the commit. 

BOTTOM:
The window on the bottom left depicts all the commit information for the selected
commit, as well the line modifications, author details, date, and other
comments. Lastly the window on the bottom right displays various views of what
was modified in the patch. In essence, the files related to the commit selected
in the bottom left window are listed.
